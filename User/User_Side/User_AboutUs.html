WELCOME TO P & Z EATERY AND EATING HOUSE



History :

P & Z EATERY AND EATING HOUSE was founded by two graduates from Belgaum, Karnataka, India in the year 2015. At the time of establishment the mess was named as P & Z EATING HOUSE but later in the year 2019 it was named to P & Z EATERY AND EATING HOUSE. Since then the busniness is running very good and its been continously trying to adapt new approaches that could satisfy all its customers and make a good progress in this field of business. At the begining after establishment, our mess provided only with the walk-in services. But from the year 2019, the mess started with parcel services and as well as delivery services to support the needs of our respected customers.





Motive :

The motive is to provide services to all its customers who don't have easy availability of food and face problems in case of food availability in their day-today life. Especially the students at college and the bachelors working in an organization face this issue on a daily basis. So, to this point, our mess will help all of them to an greater extent by providing them the services of this mess. We even provide with the service of delivery system to our customer's home on a daily basis, who find it very comfortable to have their food at their own space.





Services :

Our mess always welcomes our dear registered customers to walk-in anytime during the mess active hours. Staff at the mess take care of all the possible requirements our customers demand and try to fulfill them to the best of our possibility. At our mess we even offer Take Away Services (Parcel) to our registered customers who choose to have their food at their own space. As far as the parcel is been concerned, we make sure that the hygiene is been maintained and utmost care will be taken regarding the packing and the packkage of the food delivery. We offer Delivery Services to our registered customers who cannot visit our mess due to their tough routine schudule and who wish to have their food at their own space. As far as the parcel is been concerned, we make sure that the hygiene is been maintained and utmost care will be taken regarding the packing and the packkage of the food delivery.
