WELCOME TO P & Z EATERY AND EATING HOUSE

PZEAE at Belgaum has a wide range of products and services to cater to the varied requirements of their customers. The staff at this establishment are courteous and prompt at providing any assistance. They readily answer any queries or questions that you may have and make their customers satisfy and comfortable within 24hrs of a query reported.

Services that we offer at our eating house is as follows :
Walk-In Services ( ) :
Our mess always welcomes our dear registered customers to walk-in anytime during the mess active hours. Staff at the mess take care of all the possible requirements our customers demand and try to fulfill them to the best of our possibility.

Take Away Services ( ) :
At our mess we even offer Take Away Services (Parcel) to our registered customers who choose to have their food at their own space. As far as the parcel is been concerned, we make sure that the hygiene is been maintained and utmost care will be taken regarding the packing and the packkage of the food delivery.

Delivery Services ( ) :
We offer Delivery Services to our registered customers who cannot visit our mess due to their tough routine schudule and who wish to have their food at their own space. As far as the parcel is been concerned, we make sure that the hygiene is been maintained and utmost care will be taken regarding the packing and the packkage of the food delivery. We charge extra 50Rs per delivery of a package which will be collected by our delivery man ( In cash only ) .
